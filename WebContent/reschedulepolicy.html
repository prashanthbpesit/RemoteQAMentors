Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

Remote QA Mentors Policy for Cancellations, Rescheduling, and No-Shows (v2018.01.01)

We know that sometimes schedules change, and that unexpected and unavoidable things happen. They happen to us too! Here are our policies regarding cancellations and rescheduling classes, webinars, and other events at Remote QA Mentors.

When Remote QA Mentors needs to cancel or reschedule:

  • We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule any Remote QA Mentors service due to low enrollment or other extenuating circumstances.
  • One of the biggest reasons why Remote QA Mentors trainers are so good at what they do is that all of them are busy working in the Testing industry. However this means that on rare occasions a coach may need to reschedule a class, webinar coaching session, or other Remote QA Mentors service on short notice because of a last minute unforeseen circumstances. Should this happen to you, you will be given the option of rescheduling, a refund, or chance to join another similar Remote QA Mentors class, webinar or coaching session.
