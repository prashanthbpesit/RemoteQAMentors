About Us

Who we are, how we started this.

This is Sumith & Apurva Jain. We are part time freelancers. Over last 2 years we made more than $35,000 online part time working from home by testing simple Apps & Websites. Now we are willing to teach and mentor others on how they can do the same. We have launched a detailed 6 weeks course to teach ANYONE how to earn money from home.

We started doing so well in QA freelancing that we started getting lot of people interested to learn this and to do this. So after lot of thought and research we designed a 6 week course that can enable anyone to do what we are doing since last 2 years. This program has a single aim – To give you everything you need to succeed in making Work from Home a reality and not some abstract concept. Let us be straight forward, even in this age of digitization, there are extremely small percentage of people who are truly working from home and be their own boss! So basically by this course we give you a chance to pick our brains and to copy our success template to be able to work from home Part time or Full time.

  • Teach you basics of manual testing
  • Open your freelancing account & show you navigation
  • Help you get over online obstacles & show you the way to succeed fast
  • Train you on free apps & tools you will have to use day in and day out in freelancing world
  • Discuss on strategies, technical aspects of testing and also ways to optimize your time returns
  • Teach you how to capture logs, videos, and screenshots for raising bugs
  • Create a follow up system so that you don't lose momentum

The course is a 6 weekend course and for 3-4 hours a week. There is a specific reason for doing it once a week as in initial days you will need to learn few things and also upgrade your skills till you get frequent project invites. You will have an opportunity to withdraw from the course for a week.

There is a 1 time fee of $499 to enroll in the course. It is for the training, mentoring and for guiding you through this journey. If you just follow it, it’s not hard to make at least $10k - $25k a year.

The class strength is limited to 20 people as we don’t want to crowd it up and lose personal touch.

Our course is completely backed by 45 days Money back Guarantee. If you don’t like the course anytime, you can drop off it. We will refund you 100% of the money paid.

If you have may follow up questions please fill the demo request form and someone from our team will get in touch with you.
